Overview

For this project, I worked with three of my classmates to discuss and solve some design problems in the Yelp mobile application. During the project, my main job was to design user testing tasks, find the major problems during user testing, and give some possible solutions to my other classmates.


Testing plan

The reason that we are targeting on the Yelp mobile version is because we found that lots of our friends are using this application and facing some similar problems. For our testing users, we selected the classmates outside our design class. There are several reasons why we choose those people as our target users. First, college students are more likely to eat outside due to limited cooking skill. Second, college students are more knowledgeable on using mobile applications.
